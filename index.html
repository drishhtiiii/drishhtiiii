Hello There

I am standing on the ashes of who I used to be.

I'm 20 and skinny .
I lay hugging a pillow. I live near toys. I sit on benches and Laugh -
sometimes cry. They say , right where it began and I had never
tasted salty tears because you weren't my one and only , you were
simply mine. I didn't look at you in the crowd, you said. And trust
me I wish this was true. Your laugh echoes in my hollow heart .

I write about the things which are close to my heart üíñ

It is as if they push me back to the HELL and ask me to wake up my demons.

I see her write her emotions down on paper and she can never write words that rhyme. I admire her strength when she smiles whilst wanting to cry her teary heart. I see her embrace love even more when she was taken all hopes from. She's like the autumn leaf she says but I see her living the brightest and glow golden while she opens up her heart . She taught me love is all about giving and let it grow if you're willing to. She taught me a leaf detached from the branch , lying on cold hard ground also breathes . She taught me a love so strong will only teach you to love more . You're an autumn leaf she said . Glow golden she said.

Under the night sky, my body breaks into fragments- I'm dumb and deaf at the same time. I've made so many memories with this darkness since the day you left. My ripped heart would ache and bleed seeing myself fall that fast. I smiled, but I felt numb and I felt dead. "It'll be fine", I would hear his voice and I would be silent for hours but he would listen to things I had buried deep inside. I would ignore him for hours but he came back every time with a rose in his hand, saying "Sorry, I couldn't get a lily at the store." For the very first time I thought, I like rose. It's pretty.

Gave love about a hundred tries. Just running from demons in your mind. Then I took yours and made them mine. I didn't notice because my love was blind.

When asked about love...I feel,I remember the pain it brings but I do not cry and im not as numb as I used to be before because I know you didnt cry too . I feel the ache in my heart and I feel the hand that cares but I feel no healing now. I feel beautiful and withered but I don't feel alive now.

I found god , I found him in a lover . When his hair falls on his face and his hand so cold they shake. I found a devil , found him in a lover and his lips like tangerine and his colour coded speech. I found a saviour found him in a lover but he's off to pay his crimes and got no time for mine.

And one day if you fall out of love with me , will you pretend that we're in love ? And one day when you realise you weren't looking for flawed heart like mine , will you pretend that we're in love ? And one day when my kiss doesn't taste like drug to you , will you pretend that we're in love ? And one day when we have no more stories to discuss at your dark room where we lay naked , will you pretend we're in love ? And one day when you'll find no laughter in my smiles , will you pretend we're in love ?
